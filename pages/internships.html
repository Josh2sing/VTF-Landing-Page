Internships at VTF

Virtually Testing Foundation mission is to serve and provide on-the-job learning experience for everyone who are students, new professionals, or career changers in the cybersecurity community that will prepare them for the work force. VTF is neither selling any product/service nor client data.

Understanding of basic concepts

Our beginner internships serve as a way for students to start their journey into the professional world of cybersecurity. Beginner Interns will be required to have a high school deploma or above, a computer with internet, and must understand english fundamentals. An interview is not required, and upon completion, a certificate will be given.
